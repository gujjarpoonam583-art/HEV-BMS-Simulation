# Results Summary

## Project Outcome

The Battery Management System (BMS) simulation for Hybrid Electric Vehicle (HEV) was successfully designed and analyzed.

### Key Results
- State of Charge (SOC) monitoring implemented.
- State of Health (SOH) estimation included.
- Battery protection mechanisms considered.
- Thermal behavior analysis performed.
- Energy management dataset integrated.

### Conclusion
The proposed BMS improves battery safety, reliability, and efficiency for Hybrid Electric Vehicles.
